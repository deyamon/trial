My love,

On this day that brought you into the world, I find myself overwhelmed with gratitude — because your existence has made mine infinitely more beautiful. Every moment with you feels like a quiet miracle I didn't know I was allowed to wish for.

You make ordinary days feel sacred. You carry warmth in your laugh, depth in your eyes, and a kind of grace that makes everyone around you feel seen. I hope this birthday reminds you of how deeply you are cherished — not just today, but in every ordinary Tuesday and every long quiet evening.

Here's to you. To us. To many more years of this.

— always & entirely yours
